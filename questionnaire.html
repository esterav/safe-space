QUESTIONNAIRE
This project has been determined to be a proposal for the development of a software able to read and  analyse the data provided by the questionnaire and, with the variables, reproduce tailored environments. As a result of the investigation developed, I created a test (to be further developed by professionals of the field) that covers different aspects that need to be taken into account for reproducing the most accurate relaxation image for a user. Below is attached a reproduction of the test and the image that would appear as a result (please note that this is a demo created to offer a visual support to the idea, and the resultant images are from an archive, selected accurately after exploring studies developed by other VR platforms that helped to determine the most habitual environments to induce relaxation. In the real platform, all images would be generated by means of an image generated program).


Due to the complexity of this multiple choice with multiple results test, I have been unable to program it, so I will procede to explain the mechanism, to be developed properly by a software expert. To define the results of this test I established a punctuation system based on variables. Giving a letter to each of the possible scenarios and evaluating the punctuation of every answer to each one, the logic will be based on the sum of all answers. As a result, the variable with the highest score will appear. In case of a tie between two scenarios, the system will decide which one randomly. To see the logic of the questionnaire click here.



1. Which type of destiny do you usually go for?
a) To the north or any other cold destination.
b) To southern regions or any place where I can see the sun.

2. In which moment of the day do you feel better?
a) In the morning, I am an early bird.
b) At the afternoon, I love the dusk.
c) At night, I am a night owl.

3. When you have an issue, how do you habitually address it?
a) Taking distance and looking things in perspective, analysing the whole image.
b) Getting involved in the situation, and taking direct action in the heat of the moment.

4. You go to a mountain at the weekend. There are three options for reaching the peak: Which one will you choose?
a) Booking a 4WD and follow the road.
b) Joining an organised tour off-road.
c) Doing a high mountain trail with a couple of friends.  
5. Which are the colours that you feel more comfortable with?
a) Pastel colours (yellow, baby blue).
b) Green.
c) Light colours (white, grey or cream).
d) Dark colours (black and dark blue).
e) Vibrant colours (red, orange, purple).

6. How do you picture your perfect holidays?
a) Relaxing in a Caribbean beach.
b) Going on a road-trip around the coast of Europe.
c) Hiking in the Amazon.
d) Skiing in the Swiss Alps.
e) Camping in the middle of nowhere.

7. Which of these statements describes yourself better?
a) Listening to the sound of the water keeps me relaxed.
b) Being surrounded by wildlife and nature helps me to connect with myself.
c) Total silence. Absolutely nothing. It’s all that I need to focus.
